FAQ

Will it arrive before my viewing?

That's the whole point of the service. Standard turnaround is within 12 hours; tell us your viewing time and anything inside 24 hours jumps the queue. If we can't make your viewing, we say so before you pay.

Is this a mechanical inspection?

No — and be suspicious of anyone who claims to judge a bike's condition from listing photos. We tell you what the data says (sold prices, documented model issues) and exactly what to inspect and photograph. The in-person check is yours; for anything over $1,000, a mechanic's inspection is still worth it.

Where do the prices come from?

Actual sold listings (eBay completed sales, Pinkbike sold-flagged ads, verified buyer outcomes), shown to you comp-by-comp with source and date — plus certified-marketplace prices as a ceiling reference. Every range carries a confidence label based on how many comps we have and how fresh they are. Asking prices, when used at all, are marked and discounted. Nothing is ever invented.

Why a 30-day pass instead of paying per report?

Because that's how bike shopping actually works — you'll seriously consider a handful of bikes before one is right. The pass covers every listing in your search window, so you never skip the check on bike #4 because of another $12. Single reports exist if you're only looking at one.

What if the bike sells before my viewing?

With a pass, just send the next candidate. On a single report: if we haven't started, we move it to another listing free.
